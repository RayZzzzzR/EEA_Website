Vision Verse

  1. Home
  2. Blogs

This project was my first project that I completed under mentors in college and to be honest, it was an outstanding project. I was completely new to the field of computer vision so I took this project in the hope of learning something new. The project started off with the very basics of numpy and pandas and gradually glided is through the topics of image processing techniques. The mentors were very regular in uploading the required resources and were even forward in helping me out whenever I reached any one of them with my doubts.

They even uploaded regular assignments every week with the resources from where we can take help and those assignments helped with the grasping of the topics taught that week. The deadlines of the assignments were also not so strict so we got ample amount of time to understand them and then complete. I can now even do some self projects based on advanced topics in computer vision and image processing if I wish to, with all this knowledge that I gained with this projects.
